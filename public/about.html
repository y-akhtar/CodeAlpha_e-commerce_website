About Picksy

Welcome to Picksy, your cozy corner for curated home essentials and minimal styling decor.

Founded in 2026, Picksy was born out of a simple idea: that home accessories should be simple, functional, and classy. We believe in soft pastel tones, clean lines, and high-quality materials that transform any living space into a relaxing sanctuary.

Whether you are organizing your workspace, decorating a bedroom, or stocking your kitchen, we supply objects that represent harmony and comfort. Thank you for inviting Picksy into your home.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect standard information necessary to process your e-commerce orders, including your name, shipping address, email address, phone number, and purchase histories.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your collected contact details are used strictly to coordinate shipping and delivery dispatch. We do not sell, rent, or lease our customer databases to marketing agencies.

Picksy uses client-side localStorage to remember your active shopping bag contents and login session keys. No tracking trackers or telemetry files are injected.
